after compiling with make, the executable is produced in ./bin

launch ./bin/raytracer with one of the following arguments
it will render the according scene:
       -planet : blob representing a planet
       -atmosphere : earth like texture on a sphere with atmosphere
       -sun : same as above but with a sun
       -alien : sphere with different colors for textures and atmospheres
       -video : sun scene with camera moving around
